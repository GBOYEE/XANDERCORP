## Cold Email Deliverability (2025 Best Practices)

### Key Rules
1. **Warm up domain first** — Start with 5-10 emails/day, increase gradually
2. **Stay under limits** — Gmail: 500/day, but keep under 100 for safety
3. **Personalize** — Generic = spam. Specific = inbox
4. **Clean your list** — Remove bounces immediately
5. **Authenticate** — SPF, DKIM, DMARC records

### Timing (B2B Cold Email)
- **Best days:** Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday
- **Best times:** 9-10 AM or 3-4 PM (recipient timezone)
- **Avoid:** Monday mornings, Friday afternoons

### Email Length
- **Ideal:** 50-100 words
- **Subject line:** Under 50 characters
- **One CTA only** — Don't ask for too much

### Follow-up Strategy
- Follow up 2-3 times
- Space: 3-5 days between emails
- Change subject line each time
- Final email: "Did I get the wrong person?"

## Key Learnings

1. **Deliverability > Volume** — 50 emails that land in inbox > 200 in spam
2. **Personalization > Templates** — One good personalized email > 10 generic
3. **Follow-up wins** — 60% of replies come from 2nd-3rd follow-up
4. **Niche beats general** — "AI for Ecommerce" > "AI for everyone"
5. **ROI beats features** — "Save 15 hours/week" > "Uses GPT-4"
