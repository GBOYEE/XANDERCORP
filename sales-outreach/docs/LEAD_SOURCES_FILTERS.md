## Lead Filtering Criteria (MUST APPLY)

### Target ICP: AI Automation Clients

**✅ TARGET IF:**

| Criteria | Why It Matters |
|----------|----------------|
| Company size: 5-100 employees | Can afford $400-800, not enterprise |
| Industry: SaaS, E-commerce, Agencies | High need for automation |
| Has hiring activity (support/ops) | Pain signal = need |
| Recently funded (Series A-C) | Budget + urgency |
| Uses tech stack I know | Faster delivery |
| Located in US/UK/Europe | Better conversion rates |
| Has existing AI/automation interest | Easier sell |

**❌ SKIP IF:**

| Red Flag | Why Skip |
|----------|----------|
| No clear website or LinkedIn | Not a real business |
| Enterprise companies (1000+ employees) | Too slow, too complex |
| Very early stage (just founded) | No budget |
| Industry I can't serve | Wrong fit |
| No hiring or growth signals | No urgency |
| Located in countries with low conversion | Time waste |
| Already has automation in place | Harder sell |

## Lead Research Checklist (Before Sending)

For EACH lead, research:

- [ ] Company name + website verified
- [ ] Company size (LinkedIn/Crunchbase)
- [ ] Industry vertical
- [ ] Recent news/funding/hiring (trigger!)
- [ ] Key person's name + title
- [ ] Company pain points (from job posts, reviews)
- [ ] Current tools they use (tech stack)
- [ ] Why THEY need automation (specific!)

## Where to Find AI Automation Leads

### 1. Upwork (Primary)
- Search: "AI agent", "automation", "chatbot", "workflow automation"
- Filter: Budget $500+, 4+ stars, verified payment
- **Daily action:** Check new jobs at 9 AM + 2 PM

### 2. LinkedIn
- Search: "hiring AI automation", "looking for AI agent"
- Join: AI automation, SaaS growth, startup communities
- **Daily action:** 5 connection requests + 3 messages

### 3. Startup Databases
- Crunchbase → Recently funded companies (Series A-C)
- AngelList → Startups hiring in your niche
- **Daily action:** 5 companies researched

### 4. Job Boards (Pain Signal Mining)
- Indeed, LinkedIn Jobs → "AI", "automation", "support"
- **Signal:** Companies hiring support = pain = need

### 5. Cold Email (Own List)
- Apollo.io → Target: SaaS, 10-100 employees, US/UK
- Filter by: Uses tools like Intercom, Zapier, HubSpot
- **Daily action:** 10 verified leads researched
